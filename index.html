why love spending time in Kids Zone?

Perfect for Little Ones

We have a great area for babies and toddlers. we have dedicated, fabric swings for babies and children under 2.

Baby Fun

They have lovely sensory stuff for babies. have a little one that is less mobile, it’s nice to sit here for a while. There are some cool, squidgy Lego blocks too which are fun to build with.

Bright and Airy!

It’s bright and airy and just has a pleasant feel to it. it’s easy to navigate around the soft play bit without whacking your head or losing your toddler!

Stuff for the parents

  • There are plenty of tables, chairs and sofas for the adults. It’s handy to have a place to dump all your coats, shoes and bags.
  • Car parking. It is fine if you are a resident of the local area because you can claim parking back.
  • Great Cafe with many options and great prices.
